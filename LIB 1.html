ABOUT

The five-storeyed, air-conditioned and computerized library is well-stacked. 83000 Volumes, 400 National and International Journals, 6500 CD-ROMs, a Digital Library with 6000 e-journals, 274 NPTEL and 166 NITTTR video courses are part of the resources. BIT is an Institutional member of the British Council Library, Chennai, DELNET, New Delhi and I NDEST Consortium, New Delhi.
